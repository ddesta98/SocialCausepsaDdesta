FAST VS SLOW

Lets break it down...

Fast fashion consists of garments made with cheap quality fabrics, at a low price and always mass-produced. These machine-made garments find there way into landfills very quikcly as a couple washes means the cheap diys and poorly constructed garments don't last long. Slow fashion in contrast, is often times handmade, promotes the talent of artists, better quality and used for years.
